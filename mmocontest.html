GIMO

MMO or MetriX Mathematical Olympiads is a yearly mathematical contest aimed at a difficulty equal to IMO level. MMO was first held on year 2020 on November 4 and 5 that is day 1 on 4th and day 2 on 5th November. Registration can be done over here on AoPS

MMO (MetriX Mathematical Olympiad)

It is an IMO level contest with the usual conditions and pattern same that of IMO. It will be soon conducted on year 2021. To check its previous year you can visit here

Rules
  • Each problem is worth 7 points
  • Though several days are given for submission but you are adviced to take it in 4.5 hours
  • A geometry problem solution without a diagram will result in a one point loss irrespective of its solution.
