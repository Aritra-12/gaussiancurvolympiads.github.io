GAMO and GJMO

You can take anyone of the contests between the contests GJMO and GAMO.

GJMO (Gaussian Universal Standard Junior Mathematical Olympiad) is an USAJMO level contest with the usual conditions and pattern same that of a USAJMO contest. It will be released on two day of three problems each.
GAMO(Gaussian Universal Standard Mathematical Olympiad) is an USAMO level contest with the usual conditions and pattern same that of USAMO. It will be released on two days of three problems each.
