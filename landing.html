GAMO & GJMO

GAMO(Gaussian Universal Standard Mathematical Olympiad) and GAMO(Gaussian Universal Standard Junior Mathematical Olympiad) are intended at the level of USAMO and USAJMO. The contest is followed by GIMO(Gaussian International Mathematical Olympiad) which is an IMO standard/level Contest.

  • Learn more and Register

GIMO

Gaussian International Mathematical Olympiad is a contest aimed at International Mathematical Olympiad Level and is the next stage og GAMO and GJMO.

  • Learn more and Pre-register

MMO

An international yearly independent single stage contest aimed at IMO level and standard

  • Learn more and Pre-Register
